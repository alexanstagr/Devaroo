I was born in Cologne, Germany at 1990, and from a young age I had a passion for computers. My first computer was a Compaq Presario 5441 with an AMD K6-2 processor running at 500 MHz and just 64MB of memory. It was a gift from my father, who introduced me to the world of computing.

After studying computer technician courses and taking programming classes in high school and technical school, I realized that the internet was my true calling. That’s how my passion for frontend development and programming began. Today, I enjoy designing and building websites and apps using React, Next.js, and PHP. I also create my own plugins and extensions.

In my free time, I enjoy fishing, playing the guitar, going on day trips, and working with clay sculpting. I’ve also recently started learning Italian, about two months ago.
